# Caesium Brand Guide

## Concept

Caesium (Cs, element 55) is the element used in atomic clocks — the most precise timekeeping devices ever built. The Latin root *caesius* means "sky blue," while the metal itself is golden. Both colors anchor the brand palette.

The logomark is a stylized atom: three orbital rings in electric cyan with gold electron dots, representing distributed nodes orbiting a central scheduler.

## Typography

- **Font family**: Inter (primary), system-ui fallback
- **Wordmark**: Inter Light (300), wide letter-spacing (0.14em)
- **UI text**: Inter Regular (400) / Medium (500) / Semibold (600)

## Usage Guidelines

- **Dark-first**: The brand is designed for dark backgrounds. Always prefer the dark variant.
- **Minimum clear space**: Keep at least 1x the nucleus diameter around the icon.
- **Minimum size**: Icon should not be rendered smaller than 16x16px.
- **Do not** rotate, stretch, or recolor the logomark outside the provided variants.
- **Accent usage**: Use Caesium Blue for primary interactive elements. Use Caesium Gold sparingly for emphasis and status indicators.
